# Purging My Stuff

## Starting A Blog
So this whole project of writing a blog is new to me.
I mean, I've tried and failed a million times before. But this time, I hope, will be different.
See, it started with the realization that, as someone who is... let's say attentionally challenged, the spreading thin of my attention in all the various directions it goes without any real concerted effort in one or two areas leaves me making very little real progress. I have a lot of dreams, hopes, and interests. I've even got some talent and curiousity to back some of those things up.
